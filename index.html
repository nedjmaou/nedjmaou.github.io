Postdoctoral Researcher

I am a PhD student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology interested in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I have a Master's degree in Software Engineering and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science, University of Science and Technology Houari Boumedienne, Algiers, Algeria.

I currenly work on multiligual hate speech detection, classification and analysis under the supervision of Prof. Yangqiu Song and and Prof. Dit-Yan Yeung. Please feel free to check my curriculum vitae and my Google Scholar.
